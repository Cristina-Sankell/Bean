ABOUT US

Welcome to Bean Café!
We are three coffee-loving friends that have a passion nature and all the flavors that it has to offer. Here you can enjoy high quality coffee along with fresh food, baked goods and beverages - all organic of course! It all started when we found ourselves struggling to find cafés with organic options on the menu. Living busy lives in the city, we love taking the time to meet up some friends for a relaxing cup of coffee and a bite to eat, but it's important to us to always keep the well-being of our planet in mind. That's when the idea of Bean Café was born. We offer a place where you can spend time with friends while enjoying delicious coffee and food that are kind to the planet.

In addition to choosing organic, we always choose local when possible and we encourage you to bring your own reusable cup for your takeaway order. We reduce our use of plastic and disposable goods as much as possible and always recycle!"
